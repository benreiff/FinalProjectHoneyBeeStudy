What's the Buzz about Bee Loss?

Decline of Bees

"U.S. National Agricultural Statistics show a honey bee decline from about 6 million hives in 1947 to 2.4 million hives in 2008, a 60 percent reduction."

Why are Bees declining?

The main reasons for global bee-decline are linked to industrial agriculture, parasites/pathogens and climate change. The loss of biodiversity due to monocultures and the wide-spread use of bee-killing pesticides are particular threats for honeybees and wild pollinators.

Cleaning with Pandas

We cleaned the data with Pandas. We combinded rows, changed headings and deleted uneccessary elements. Our data came from the USDA website for agricultural studies.

Machine Learning

achine learning is an application of artificial intelligence (AI) that provides systems the ability to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed. Machine learning focuses on the development of computer programs that can access data and use it learn for themselves.

Tableau

We created a dashbaord that shows visuals of honey production, honey value by state, honey production measured by money and honey production pounds by country with the countries population. These maps have a legend and are interactive. Tableau is a powerful and fastest growing data visualization tool used in the Business Intelligence Industry. It helps in simplifying raw data into the very easily understandable format. Data analysis is very fast with Tableau and the visualizations created are in the form of dashboards and worksheets.

HTML/CSS

We created a website with animations using advanced CSS. Our website follows the BEM model with SASS properties.
We have imbedded our Tableau dashboard and have interative buttons and a responsive navigation bar.
