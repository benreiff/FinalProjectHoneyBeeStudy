Do We Have a Plan Bee? Analyzing 30 Years of Honey Data

What's the Buzz about Bee Loss?

Decline of Bees

"U.S. National Agricultural Statistics show a honey bee decline from about 6 million hives in 1947 to 2.4 million hives in 2008, a 60 percent reduction."

Why are Bees declining?

The main reasons for the decline in bee population can be linked to industrial agriculture, parasites/pathogens and climate change. The loss of biodiversity due to monocultures and widespread use of bee killing pesticides are particular threats for honey bees and wild pollinators.
